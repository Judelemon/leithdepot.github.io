Leith Depot

We have a very cosy, welcoming and laid back bar located on Leith Walk, with lovely huge windows looking out onto lovely Leith Walk. Our fantastic Team at Leith Depot are very friendly and are happy to ensure that you have an enjoyable experience.

At Leith Depot we like to think that there’s something for everyone. We supply great bar snacks, tea and artisan coffee. We have a wide selection of bottled craft beers & wines and numerous draughts to choose from.

On our food menu we serve a largely pescatarian and vegetarian menu but there is always an option for the carnivores amongst you.
