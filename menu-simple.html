Restaurant Details

We serve a largely pescatarian and vegetarian menu at Leith Depot but there’s always an option for the carnivores amongst you. Our food is of restaurant quality but at bar prices. We use local food suppliers and fishmongers and so our menu can change seasonally.
We pride oursevles on providing nutritious, healthy, wholesome and tasty options and you can choose from our light bites, starters or mains.

Our food service

We’re pretty busy on food service Thursday, Friday, Saturday & Sundays so to avoid disappointment make your reservations or enquires via email to us at leithdepot@gmail.com, facebook message us or give us a bell on 0131 555 4738
Please provide; Name/date/time/contact number/number of people

12pm - 9pm Sunday, Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday.
12pm – 10pm Thursday, Friday & Saturday
We have a small kitchen so sometimes things may take a little longer especially if food service is busy and so pre-orders are welcome for larger groups or if you’re stuck for time just let us know.

Leith Depot : Bar - Restaurant - Venue
We have a very cosy, welcoming and laid back bar located on Leith Walk, with lovely huge windows looking out onto lovely Leith Walk. Our fantastic Team at Leith Depot are very friendly and are happy to ensure that you have an enjoyable experience.
